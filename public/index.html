Liar's Dice Tracker

Rules

  • Each player starts with five dice.
  • All players roll their dice simultaneously and keep them hidden.
  • The starting player makes a bid (quantity + face value).
  • The next player must either raise the bid or call it.
  • A raised bid be a higher quantity or use a higher face value for the same quantity.
  • If called, reveal all dice and tally the dice with the bid's face value.
  • Ones are wild, and are included in the tally, regardless of the face value in the bid.
  • If the tally is at least the bid, the bidder wins; otherwise the caller wins.
  • The loser of the showdown loses one die; players with zero dice are eliminated.
